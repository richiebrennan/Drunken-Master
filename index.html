About Drunken Master

Located in Kyungsung, Drunken Master is the first and only place to offer American-style Chinese food in Busan. Along with our amazing selection of cocktails, friendly atmosphere and service, we offer the best dining experience in the city. Come down and enjoy a taste of home.
